About the Nikerum Syndicate

Who are we?

We are more of a group, rather than a company or corporation (not even a shell corporation, thankfully), as currently, we have yet to be formally founded. And to add to that, this "group" only contains one person, ONE PERSON, and it's the founder, who's currently looking for members, or "employees". However, we aim to be a company that makes games, and maybe software, and many other things in different fields. (Also yes, we are currently thinking of fixing up a city from the ground up. Probably Detroit.)

And yes, we'll try to compete with Kadokawa Corporation in the future.

Besides being a software company, we also set our sites on making some short films (usually for promotional purposes, or entertainment purposes) with as much effort as possible, while on a low budget. We also want to open a restaurant, a seafood restaurant that serves mostly shrimp to be specific. See "Restaurant Cities" for planned locations.
